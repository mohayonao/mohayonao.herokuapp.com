Web Cam Theremin

Operation

  1. click "Allow" to enable access to your webcam
  2. right-hand's Y position: frequency
  3. left-hand's Y position: amplitude

Usging

  • WebAudioAPI / AudioDataAPI (play sound)
  • WebWorker (detect hand positions)
  • WebRTC (Google Chrome Canary build with flags:enable-media-stream)
  • jQuery webcam plugin
